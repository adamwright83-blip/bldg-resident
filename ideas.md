# Laundry Butler - Design Approach

## Selected Approach: Faithful Replication of User's Design

The user has provided a complete, pixel-perfect mockup with all assets. The design philosophy is already established:

**Design Movement**: Old-world luxury / Private club aesthetic
**Core Principles**: Restraint, exclusivity, quiet confidence, serif elegance
**Color Philosophy**: Monochromatic — warm off-white textured paper background, black text, black CTA buttons
**Layout Paradigm**: Two-column hero and service sections, centered pricing section
**Signature Elements**: Shield/crest logo with laurel wreath, decorative olive branches, textured linen paper background
**Typography System**: Classic serif pairing — Playfair Display for display headings, Cormorant Garamond for body
**Interaction Philosophy**: Minimal — the luxury speaks through restraint, not animation

This is a direct execution of the user's vision. No creative liberties will be taken.
